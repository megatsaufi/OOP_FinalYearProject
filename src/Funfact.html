Diwali Festival

Date: October 24, 2024

Location: Mumbai, India

Description: Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is one of the most important Hindu festivals. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. The celebration includes lighting oil lamps, fireworks, exchanging sweets, and prayers to the goddess Lakshmi for prosperity.

Oktoberfest

Date: September 21 - October 6, 2024

Location: Munich, Germany

Description: Oktoberfest is the world's largest beer festival held annually in Munich, Germany. It is a 16- to 18-day folk festival running from late September to the first weekend in October. Visitors enjoy traditional Bavarian food, music, and of course, plenty of beer.

Carnival of Rio de Janeiro

Date: February 21 - February 26, 2024

Location: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Description: The Carnival of Rio de Janeiro is the world's most famous carnival. It is a festival held before Lent every year and considered the biggest carnival in the world with two million people per day on the streets. The event features samba parades, street parties, and vibrant costumes.

Cherry Blossom Festival

Date: March 20 - April 14, 2024

Location: Tokyo, Japan

Description: The Cherry Blossom Festival, known as Hanami in Japan, celebrates the beauty of cherry blossoms. It is a time when families and friends gather under cherry blossom trees to enjoy picnics and celebrate the arrival of spring. The festival also includes traditional Japanese music and dance performances.

Mardi Gras

Date: February 25, 2024

Location: New Orleans, United States

Description: Mardi Gras, also known as Fat Tuesday, is a carnival celebration that marks the day before the fasting season of Lent. It is celebrated with parades, music, masquerade balls, and colorful floats. Mardi Gras is famous for its festive atmosphere and elaborate costumes.
